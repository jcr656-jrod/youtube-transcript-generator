   a) Create new repository at https://github.com/new

   a) Visit https://railway.app

   c) Click "New Project"

   e) Select your repository
   f) Authorize Railway.app

Step 3: CONFIGURE ENVIRONMENT VARIABLES
   OPENAI_API_KEY=***

   STRIPE_SECRET_KEY=***

   ENVIRONMENT=production
   DEBUG=false
